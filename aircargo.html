Urgent and Time Sensitive Cargo

When time-critical cargo must go now to include food, perishables, medical supplies, and live animals.

Heavy and Outsized or Irregular Cargo

So what is oversize freight? Well, simply put, its cargo, which cannot fit on standardized transport devices such as skids (pallets) or 40′ shipping containers. This includes vehicles, boats, trailers, trucks, light aircraft, industrial machinery, and military equipment.

Dangerous and Hazardous Goods

Hazardous substances are classified based only on health effects (whether they are immediate or long-term), while dangerous goods are classified according to their immediate physical or chemical effects, such as fire, explosion, corrosion and poisoning, affecting property, the environment or people.

Remote Destinations and Conflict Zones

Support for humanitarian aid, disaster relief and military operations in remote locations or conflict zones.
